Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a soft baked crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings. It is loved around the world for its cheesy texture and delicious flavors.

This classic dish can be customized with vegetables, chicken, or other toppings, making it a favorite meal for parties and family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Onion slices
  • Capsicum slices
  • Tomato slices
  • Olives
  • Italian seasoning
  • Chili flakes
  • Olive oil
  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Roll the pizza dough into a circle.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Place vegetables and other toppings evenly.
  6. Sprinkle Italian seasoning and chili flakes.
  7. Bake the pizza until the crust turns golden.
  8. Remove from the oven and slice.
  9. Serve hot.
