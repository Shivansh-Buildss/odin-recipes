Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy North Indian dish made with tender chicken cooked in a mildly spiced tomato-based gravy. It is famous for its buttery texture, smoky flavor, and slightly sweet taste that pairs perfectly with naan or rice.

This dish was originally created in Delhi and has become one of the most loved Indian curries around the world. The combination of cream, butter, and aromatic spices makes it comforting and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the chicken in a pan until lightly browned.
  3. Heat butter in another pan and add tomato puree.
  4. Add spices and cook the gravy for a few minutes.
  5. Add the cooked chicken to the gravy.
  6. Mix in fresh cream and simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
