Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with fragrant basmati rice, marinated chicken, and aromatic spices. It is known for its rich taste, beautiful layers, and delicious aroma.

This popular dish is often prepared during celebrations and family gatherings. Every bite contains spicy chicken and perfectly cooked rice infused with herbs and spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala
  • Mint leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices.
  3. Fry onions until golden brown.
  4. Cook tomatoes and spices in a large pot.
  5. Add chicken and cook until tender.
  6. Partially cook the rice separately.
  7. Layer rice and chicken mixture in the pot.
  8. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  9. Serve hot with raita.
