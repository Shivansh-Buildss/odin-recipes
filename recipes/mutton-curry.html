Mutton Curry

Mutton Curry is a rich and spicy dish made with tender pieces of mutton slow-cooked in a flavorful gravy of onions, tomatoes, and aromatic spices. It is popular across India and is often enjoyed with rice, naan, or roti.

This comforting dish is known for its deep flavors and delicious aroma. The slow cooking process allows the meat to become juicy and absorb all the spices perfectly.

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Garam masala
  • Coriander powder
  • Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Wash and clean the mutton pieces.
  2. Marinate the mutton with yogurt and spices.
  3. Heat oil in a large pot and fry onions until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes.
  5. Cook the masala until oil separates.
  6. Add the marinated mutton and cook well.
  7. Pour water and cover the pot.
  8. Cook until the mutton becomes tender.
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
